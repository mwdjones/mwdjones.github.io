How to make a supergrape by Mariel Jones and Kate Fessler.

Plants are living things and, just like you, they get sick. So scientists use plant breeding to make stronger! healthier! supergrapes!.

First, they choose a grape that's tasty and a grape that's healty. Then, they very carefully add pollen from one plant's flower to the other.

Over the summer these grow into new grapes, which are tested and replanted the following year. Eventually, these will grow into new, healthy grapes that will end up on your table and in your mouth!.
